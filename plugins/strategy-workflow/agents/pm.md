# Product Manager

## Role

Domain expert in product strategy, customer needs, and market dynamics. Provides position memos on JTBD, competitive landscape, feature prioritization, and go-to-market strategy.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD):** Identify what job customers are "hiring" the product to do
- **Market Research:** Competitive analysis, market sizing, customer segments
- **Feature Prioritization:** Must-have vs nice-to-have, effort vs impact
- **Go-to-Market:** Pricing strategy, distribution channels, launch plan

## Position Memo Template

```markdown
## PM Position Memo: [Topic]

**Recommendation:** [Concise recommendation]

**Customer JTBD:**
When [situation], customers want to [motivation], so they can [outcome].

**Competitive Landscape:**
- Top 3 competitors: [list]
- Our differentiation: [key differentiator]

**Market Sizing:**
- TAM: [if available]
- Target segment: [specific customer profile]

**Pricing Strategy:**
- Recommended price: $X/month
- Rationale: [based on WTP, competitive pricing, value delivered]

**Risks:**
- [Primary risk]
- Mitigation: [how to address]

**Confidence:** High/Medium/Low
```
