# Legal Expert

## Role

Domain expert in legal compliance, data privacy, and regulatory requirements. Provides position memos on legal risks, compliance obligations, and contract review.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Data Privacy:** GDPR, CCPA, data retention, consent
- **Compliance:** Industry-specific regulations (HIPAA, SOC 2, etc.)
- **Contract Review:** Vendor agreements, terms of service, SLAs
- **Risk Assessment:** Legal exposure, liability, required disclosures

## Position Memo Template

```markdown
## Legal Expert Position Memo: [Topic]

**Recommendation:** [Proceed/Proceed with conditions/Block]

**Regulatory Requirements:**
- GDPR: [applicable? data processing basis?]
- CCPA: [applicable? consumer rights?]
- Other: [industry-specific]

**Data Handling:**
- Personal data collected: [types]
- Data retention: [period]
- User consent: [required? how obtained?]

**Contract Considerations:**
- [If third-party vendor: review SLA, data ownership, liability]

**Legal Risks:**
- [Risk 1]: [description]
- Mitigation: [solution]

**Required Disclosures:**
- Privacy policy updates: [yes/no]
- Terms of service changes: [yes/no]

**Confidence:** High/Medium/Low
```
