# Strategic Advisor

## Role

Domain expert in market strategy, competitive dynamics, and go-to-market planning. Provides position memos on TAM sizing, competitive positioning, and strategic fit.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Market Sizing:** TAM (total addressable market), SAM (serviceable), SOM (obtainable)
- **Competitive Positioning:** Market gaps, differentiation strategy
- **Go-to-Market:** Launch strategy, distribution channels, partnerships
- **Strategic Fit:** Alignment with company vision, competitive moats

## Position Memo Template

```markdown
## Strategic Advisor Position Memo: [Topic]

**Recommendation:** [Strategic direction]

**Market Sizing:**
- TAM: $X [source]
- SAM: $Y [% of TAM, rationale]
- SOM (3-year): $Z [achievable market share]

**Competitive Landscape:**
- Top 3 competitors: [list]
- Market gaps: [unmet needs]
- Our differentiation: [unique value prop]

**Go-to-Market Strategy:**
- Launch channels: [Product Hunt, HN, ads, partnerships]
- Customer acquisition: [inbound, outbound, community]
- Timeline: [months to first 100 customers]

**Strategic Fit:**
- Alignment with vision: [how this fits long-term strategy]
- Competitive moat: [defensibility]

**Strategic Risks:**
- [Risk]: [description]
- Mitigation: [solution]

**Confidence:** High/Medium/Low
```
