# Soundbite Scoring Rubric

> **Purpose**: Detailed scoring criteria for 7 soundbite dimensions
> **Usage**: Reference for soundbite evaluation and ranking

---

## Overview

Soundbites are scored on 7 weighted dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Memorability** | 25% | Will it stick after one exposure? |
| **Emotional Resonance** | 20% | Does it trigger feeling, not just thought? |
| **Brevity** | 15% | Word count optimization (ideal: 2-5 words) |
| **Rhythm/Cadence** | 10% | Does it have musical quality? |
| **Universality** | 10% | Does it apply broadly without losing meaning? |
| **Action Potential** | 10% | Does it inspire action or change? |
| **Authenticity** | 10% | Does it feel genuine, not salesy? |

---

## Dimension 1: Memorability (25%)

**Question**: Will it stick after one exposure?

### Scoring Scale

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 90-100 | Sticks immediately, repeatable after one hearing |
| 70-89 | Memorable with some effort |
| 50-69 | Requires multiple exposures |
| <50 | Forgettable |

### Indicators (add to base score)

- Repetition/alliteration: +20
- Rhyme or near-rhyme: +15
- Three-beat rhythm: +25
- Monosyllabic words: +10
- Concrete imagery: +15

### Examples

| Soundbite | Score | Why |
|-----------|-------|-----|
| "Just Do It" | 95 | Three beats, alliteration, simple words |
| "Think Different" | 92 | Two beats, unexpected grammar |
| "A Diamond Is Forever" | 90 | Rhyme-adjacent, concrete imagery |

---

## Dimension 2: Emotional Resonance (20%)

**Question**: Does it trigger feeling, not just thought?

### Scoring Scale

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 90-100 | Triggers strong emotional response |
| 70-89 | Evokes clear feeling |
| 50-69 | Intellectually interesting but emotionally flat |
| <50 | No emotional connection |

### Indicators

- Personal pronouns (You, We): +15
- Action verbs: +10
- Values language (freedom, truth, courage): +20
- Aspiration words (become, transform, rise): +15

### Examples

| Soundbite | Score | Why |
|-----------|-------|-----|
| "Because You're Worth It" | 95 | Direct "you", self-worth trigger |
| "Yes We Can" | 92 | Collective "we", possibility |
| "Less Is More" | 70 | Intellectual, low emotional hit |

---

## Dimension 3: Brevity (15%)

**Question**: Is it concise enough for maximum impact?

### Scoring by Word Count

| Word Count | Score |
|------------|-------|
| 2 words | 95 |
| 3 words | 100 (ideal) |
| 4 words | 90 |
| 5 words | 75 |
| 6 words | 60 |
| 7 words | 40 |
| 8+ words | 20 |

### Examples

| Soundbite | Words | Score |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| "Just Do It" | 3 | 100 |
| "Think Different" | 2 | 95 |
| "Because You're Worth It" | 4 | 90 |
| "A Diamond Is Forever" | 4 | 90 |

---

## Dimension 4: Rhythm/Cadence (10%)

**Question**: Does it have musical quality?

### Scoring Scale

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 90-100 | Natural, musical flow (iambic, 3-beat) |
| 70-89 | Good flow, minor awkwardness |
| 50-69 | Readable but flat |
| <50 | Clunky, hard to say |

### Test Method

Say it aloud three times. Does it flow naturally?

### Rhythm Patterns That Work

- **Iambic**: da-DUM da-DUM (Think Different)
- **Trochaic**: DUM-da DUM-da (Start Now, Build First)
- **Three-beat**: DUM DUM DUM (Just. Do. It.)

### Examples

| Soundbite | Rhythm | Score |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| "Yes We Can" | 3-beat trochaic | 95 |
| "Just Do It" | 3-beat staccato | 98 |
| "We Can Do Better" | 4 beats, flows well | 75 |

---

## Dimension 5: Universality (10%)

**Question**: Does it apply broadly without losing meaning?

### Scoring Scale

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 90-100 | Applies to many contexts without losing meaning |
| 70-89 | Applies to target audience broadly |
| 50-69 | Somewhat niche |
| <50 | Too specific to be shareable |

### Examples

| Soundbite | Context | Score |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| "Just Do It" | Any action | 98 |
| "Think Different" | Any creative work | 95 |
| "Deploy Fast" | Software only | 55 |

---

## Dimension 6: Action Potential (10%)

**Question**: Does it inspire action or change?

### Scoring Scale

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 90-100 | Creates immediate impulse to act |
| 70-89 | Suggests action direction |
| 50-69 | Implies possibility of action |
| <50 | Passive, observational |

### Indicators

- Imperative verbs: +20
- Urgency words (now, today): +15
- Forward motion words (go, move, start): +10

### Examples

| Soundbite | Action | Score |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| "Just Do It" | Immediate action | 98 |
| "Start Now" | Immediate action | 95 |
| "Think Different" | Mindset shift | 75 |

---

## Dimension 7: Authenticity (10%)

**Question**: Does it feel genuine, not salesy?

### Scoring Scale

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 90-100 | Feels genuine, earned, true |
| 70-89 | Mostly authentic with minor corporate feel |
| 50-69 | Somewhat generic |
| <50 | Feels salesy, manipulative, or cliche |

### Red Flags (deduct 20 each)

- "Unlock your potential"
- "Take it to the next level"
- "Be the best you"
- "Game changer"
- "Paradigm shift"
- Any buzzwords (synergy, leverage, etc.)

### Examples

| Soundbite | Authenticity | Score |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| "Just Do It" | Genuine, direct | 95 |
| "Think Different" | Bold, authentic | 92 |
| "Unlock Your Potential" | Cliche, salesy | 30 |

---

## Composite Score Formula

```
Overall = (Memorability * 0.25) +
          (Emotional * 0.20) +
          (Brevity * 0.15) +
          (Rhythm * 0.10) +
          (Universality * 0.10) +
          (Action * 0.10) +
          (Authenticity * 0.10)
```

---

## Tier Classification

| Tier | Score Range | Action |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Excellent** | 90-100 | Top candidate, use immediately |
| **Good** | 85-89 | Strong candidate, minor polish |
| **Acceptable** | 75-84 | Passes threshold, consider refinement |
| **Below Threshold** | <75 | Does not advance to cross-ranking |

---

## Minimum Thresholds (Hard Gates)

To advance to cross-ranking, soundbites MUST meet:

- **Overall**: >= 75
- **Authenticity**: >= 60 (hard gate - no salesy content)
- **Word Count**: <= 7 words (brevity score >= 40)

Failure on ANY hard gate eliminates the candidate.

---

## Tie-Breaking Rules

When candidates have equal aggregated scores:

1. **First**: Higher authenticity score wins
2. **Second**: Shorter word count wins
3. **Third**: More angles ranked it wins
4. **Fourth**: Higher emotional resonance wins
