# Interview Toolkit

Structured discovery call support and post-interview synthesis for consultants, coaches, and service providers.

## Skills

### discovery-call
Real-time interview assistant — structured note-taking, follow-up question suggestions, pain point extraction, and time management during live calls.

### interview-synthesis
Post-interview synthesis — transforms raw interview notes into actionable deliverables (quick win recommendations, customized guides, ranked automation/solution ideas, and pilot recommendations).

## Workflow

1. **During call**: Use `discovery-call` for real-time support
2. **After call**: Use `interview-synthesis` to generate deliverables

The discovery call skill outputs structured notes that feed directly into the synthesis skill.

## Who This Is For

- Consultants running discovery calls with prospects
- Coaches conducting intake interviews
- Service providers scoping new engagements
- Anyone who needs structured interview capture and follow-up deliverables
