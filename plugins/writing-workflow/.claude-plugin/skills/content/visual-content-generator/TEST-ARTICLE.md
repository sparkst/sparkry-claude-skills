# AI Adoption Framework for Pharma Teams

Your team is split on AI adoption. Half are building, half won't touch it. Most leaders treat this as a people problem. It's not. It's a system design problem.

## The Permission Architecture

I built a three-layer framework for regulated teams navigating AI adoption. Here's how it works:

### The Three Layers

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                  PERMISSION ARCHITECTURE                     │
│                  (System Design for AI Adoption)             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

    ┌───────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │     1. DECISION RIGHTS                    │
    │  ┌─────────────────────────────────┐      │
    │  │ Where can AI decide, suggest,   │      │
    │  │ or just research?               │      │
    │  └─────────────────────────────────┘      │
    └──────────────┬────────────────────────────┘
                   │
                   ▼
    ┌───────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │     2. QUALITY CONTRACTS                  │
    │  ┌─────────────────────────────────┐      │
    │  │ What does "good enough" mean?   │      │
    │  └─────────────────────────────────┘      │
    └──────────────┬────────────────────────────┘
                   │
                   ▼
    ┌───────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │     3. HANDOFF PROTOCOLS                  │
    │  ┌─────────────────────────────────┐      │
    │  │ APIs between AI & non-AI work   │      │
    │  └─────────────────────────────────┘      │
    └───────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Layer 1: Decision Rights** - Define where AI can decide, suggest, or just research. One pharma team made AI "decision-capable" for internal docs, "suggestion-only" for regulatory submissions. Clear boundaries. Both adopters and skeptics knew the rules.

**Layer 2: Quality Contracts** - What does "good enough" mean when AI is involved? For compliance docs: AI draft + two human reviewers + automated regulatory checks. This gave skeptics safety and adopters a path forward.

**Layer 3: Handoff Protocols** - What happens when AI work moves to non-AI users? Content teams tagged AI drafts with prompt used and key facts checked. Non-AI editors knew exactly what to verify.

If doubling AI usage in 30 days would break your system, you don't have adoption infrastructure. You have adoption theater.
