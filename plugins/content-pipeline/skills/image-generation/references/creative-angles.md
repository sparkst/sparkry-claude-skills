# Creative Angles for Image Variations

> **Purpose**: Load when generating multiple variations of a concept. Each angle produces a distinct visual direction.

## The 8 Angles

### 1. Emotional Warmth
**Focus**: Human emotion and connection
**Scene**: Person in comfortable setting, AI as gentle presence
**Modifiers**: Warm lighting, genuine smile, intimate setting, personal moment
**Best for**: Articles about human impact, adoption stories, personal transformation
**Psychology**: Faces with genuine emotion get 38% more engagement (Georgia Tech research)

### 2. Dramatic Contrast
**Focus**: Juxtapose chaos/calm or old/new
**Scene**: Split composition — left: problem/struggle, right: AI-assisted solution
**Modifiers**: Dramatic lighting, strong shadows, visual tension, split frame
**Best for**: Before/after stories, problem-solution articles, paradigm shifts
**Psychology**: Contrast creates cognitive processing, which increases memorability

### 3. Aspirational Freedom
**Focus**: Freedom and possibility enabled by AI
**Scene**: Person in paradise while AI handles the work
**Modifiers**: Open sky, beach/nature, expansive view, golden hour, relaxed posture
**Best for**: Lifestyle transformation, work-life balance, automation benefits
**Psychology**: Aspirational imagery triggers dopamine; beach/nature settings reduce viewer stress

### 4. Determined Builder
**Focus**: Human actively building with AI assistance
**Scene**: Person creating/tinkering with AI augmenting their capability
**Modifiers**: Workshop/garage, tools, focused expression, blue-collar setting
**Best for**: How-to content, technical tutorials, building/creating narratives
**Psychology**: Competence imagery triggers admiration and desire to emulate

### 5. Discovery / Wonder
**Focus**: First contact / breakthrough moment
**Scene**: Person encountering AI capability for the first time
**Modifiers**: Wide eyes, reaching hand, glowing interface, sense of awe
**Best for**: New technology announcements, paradigm-shifting discoveries, innovation stories
**Psychology**: Surprise is the highest-sharing emotion; awe increases perceived value

### 6. Everyday Magic
**Focus**: AI seamlessly integrated into mundane life
**Scene**: Ordinary scene with AI working invisibly in background
**Modifiers**: Kitchen, commute, morning coffee, casual setting, subtle AI presence
**Best for**: Practical AI applications, daily workflow improvements, accessibility stories
**Psychology**: Relatable settings create identification; viewers see themselves in the scene

### 7. Comic Narrative
**Focus**: Story told in panels with humor
**Scene**: 2-3 panel comic with cartoon characters and punchline
**Modifiers**: Clean cartoon style, speech bubbles, step labels, friendly robots
**Best for**: Funny takes, industry commentary, explainers, memes
**Psychology**: Humor increases shareability 3x; sequential narrative creates completion desire

### 8. Cinematic Epic
**Focus**: Movie-poster scale and drama
**Scene**: Person standing before massive AI presence, heroic framing
**Modifiers**: Low angle, dramatic sky, large-scale AI, epic proportions
**Best for**: Big announcements, industry shifts, transformative moments
**Psychology**: Low-angle shots convey power; epic scale triggers awe response

## Angle Selection Guide

| Article Type | Primary Angle | Secondary Angle |
|-------------|---------------|-----------------|
| News/breaking | Cinematic Epic | Dramatic Contrast |
| How-to/tutorial | Determined Builder | Everyday Magic |
| Opinion/commentary | Dramatic Contrast | Comic Narrative |
| Personal story | Emotional Warmth | Discovery Wonder |
| Product/tool review | Everyday Magic | Aspirational Freedom |
| Strategic/business | Aspirational Freedom | Cinematic Epic |
| Humor/meme | Comic Narrative | Discovery Wonder |

## Variation Diversity Rules

When generating N variations:
- NEVER use the same human demographics twice
- NEVER use the same setting twice
- Vary between 3D/photorealistic/comic styles
- At least one variation should be "unexpected" — furthest from the obvious interpretation
- Each variation should evoke a DIFFERENT emotion
